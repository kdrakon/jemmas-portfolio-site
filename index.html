My grandfather gave me his old SLR when I was 14 years old. I was immediately hooked. Although I've upgraded my camera since then, I still have and use his when I want to reminisce with film photography, a medium that should never be lost.

During university, graphic design became an equally important passion in my life. Where photography gave me the ability to capture, graphic design gives me the power to create and explore so many more areas.

Together, they are a career that I love.
